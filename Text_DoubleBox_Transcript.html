You meet a famous cricketer for the first time. Ask him/her a few questions.

A: Hello. You are Viraj, the cricketer, aren't you?
B: Yes, I am.
A: We are seeing you here for the first time. What brings you here?
B: I have come to play a match against your village team. They invited me.
A: When is your next big match?
B: I have a big match next week. I will be going to England to play.
A: Do you feel proud of representing India?
B: Yes, I feel very lucky to represent my great country.
